About Luo City Spa Club

Relax, Recharge & Rejuvenate by the Manila Bay shoreline

Our Story

Luo City Spa Club was created to be a quiet escape in the middle of the city — a place where professionals, families, and travellers can pause, breathe, and reconnect with themselves. Overlooking the scenic Esplanade Seaside Terminal in the MOA Complex, our spa blends modern wellness with the calming energy of Manila Bay.

From the moment you arrive, our team is focused on one thing: your comfort. Every treatment, scent, sound and space is thoughtfully designed to help you feel restored, renewed and ready for what comes next.

A Calm Space in the City

Soft lighting, warm interiors and attentive service come together to create a sanctuary where you can slow down — even just for a few hours.

What Makes Us Different

Thoughtful Treatments

From classic massages to signature Luo City rituals, our offerings are curated to ease tension, calm the mind and promote better rest.

Seaside Location

Located at Esplanade Seaside Terminal, our spa lets you enjoy gentle bay breezes and city lights — a unique backdrop to your wellness experience.

Warm, Local Hospitality

Our team takes pride in Filipino hospitality — attentive, respectful and always focused on making you feel genuinely cared for.

Our Commitment

Luo City Spa Club is committed to providing a clean, safe and relaxing environment for every guest. We follow strict hygiene standards, use trusted spa products, and continuously train our therapists to ensure you receive consistent, quality care.
